Mercury

Smallest and closest to the Sun, Mercury has no moons and experiences extreme temperature swings.

Moons and Rovers

Mercury has no natural moons.

No rovers have been sent to Mercury. However, NASA's MESSENGER spacecraft orbited Mercury from 2011 to 2015, mapping its surface and studying its magnetic field.

List of Mercury's Moons | Learn more about MESSENGER

Suitability for Life

Due to its lack of atmosphere and extreme temperature fluctuations, Mercury is not suitable for living beings in the present or foreseeable future.
