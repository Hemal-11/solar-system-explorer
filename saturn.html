Saturn

Celebrated for its stunning ring system and Titan, its largest moon with a thick atmosphere.

Moons and Rovers

Saturn has over 80 moons, including Titan, the second largest moon in the solar system, which has a dense atmosphere.

No rovers have landed on Saturn, but the Cassini spacecraft orbited Saturn from 2004 to 2017, providing detailed images and data about the planet and its moons.

Learn about Titan | Learn about Cassini-Huygens

Suitability for Life

Saturn itself is inhospitable to life. Titan, however, is a key target in the search for life due to its complex chemistry and subsurface ocean potential.
