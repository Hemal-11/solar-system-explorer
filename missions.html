Important Space Missions

  • Apollo 11: First crewed Moon landing mission (1969).
  • Voyager 1 & 2: Probes exploring outer planets and interstellar space (1977).
  • Mariner 4: First Mars flyby, transmitted first close-up Mars images (1965).
  • Curiosity Rover: Mars rover exploring Gale Crater geology (2012-present).
  • International Space Station (ISS): Modular space habitat and research station (2000-present).
  • Chandrayaan-2: India's lunar orbiter and lander mission (2019).
  • New Horizons: Pluto flyby and Kuiper Belt exploration (2015).
  • Pioneer 10: First spacecraft to pass through the asteroid belt and make direct observations of Jupiter (1972).
  • Mars Express: ESA Mars orbiter studying surface & atmosphere (2003-present).
  • Hubble Space Telescope: Wide-ranging astronomical observatory in low Earth orbit (1990-present).
  • Galileo: Jupiter orbiter that studied Jovian system and moons (1995-2003).
  • Mars Phoenix: Polar lander that studied ice and soil (2008).
  • Juno: Currently studying Jupiter's structure and magnetosphere (2016-present).
  • Spirit & Opportunity: Twin Mars rovers exploring geology and searching for water evidence (2004-2019).
  • ExoMars: ESA/Roscosmos Mars exploration program (ongoing).
  • Chang'e 4: First soft landing on Moon's far side by China (2019).
  • Phoenix Mars Lander: Mars polar region study (2008).
  • Mangalyaan (Mars Orbiter Mission): India's Mars orbiter, first successful Indian interplanetary mission (2013).
  • Venera 7: First spacecraft to land on another planet â€” Venus (1970).
  • OSIRIS-REx: NASA asteroid sample return mission (2016-present).
  • Luna 2: First human-made object to impact the Moon (1959).
  • Luna 9: First spacecraft to achieve lunar soft landing (1966).
  • Mariner 9: First spacecraft to orbit another planet (Mars, 1971).
  • MESSENGER: Orbited Mercury to map and analyze its surface (2011-2015).
  • Cassini-Huygens: Long-term Saturn orbiter and Titan lander (1997-2017).
  • Hayabusa: JAXA's asteroid sample return mission (2003-2010).
  • Hayabusa2: Follow-up asteroid sample return (2014-present).
  • Phobos-Grunt: Russian mission to Phobos moon of Mars (2011, failed landing).
  • Spirit Rover: Mars surface exploration (2004-2010).
  • Opportunity Rover: Mars surface exploration (2004-2019).
  • Perseverance Rover: Current Mars rover searching for signs of past life (2020-present).
  • Viking 1: First successful Mars landing with surface experiments (1976-1982).
  • Viking 2: Second Viking Mars mission with lander and orbiter (1976-1980).
  • Lunar Orbiter Program: Mapped the Moon for Apollo landings (1966-1967).
  • Pioneer 11: Explored Jupiter and Saturn, first to encounter Saturn (1973-1995).
  • Spitzer Space Telescope: Infrared space observatory (2003-2020).
  • Kepler Space Telescope: Discovered thousands of exoplanets (2009-2018).
  • James Webb Space Telescope: Advanced infrared observatory launched in 2021.
  • Venera 13: Venus surface lander that transmitted photos and analyses (1982).
  • Spirit Rover: Explored Mars surface geology (2004-2010).
  • Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO): Mapping lunar surface and resources (2009-present).
  • Chang'e 5: Sample return mission from the Moon (2020).
  • Akatsuki: JAXA's Venus climate orbiter (2010-present).
  • Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM): India's first Mars mission (2013-present).
  • Deep Space 1: Technology demonstration mission (1998-2001).
  • Mir Space Station: Russian modular space station (1986-2001).
  • GRACE: Mapped Earth's gravity field (2002-2017).
  • Clementine: Lunar and asteroid imaging mission (1994).
  • OSIRIS-REx: Asteroid sample return mission to Bennu (2016-present).
  • DSCOVR: Earth observation and solar wind monitoring (2015-present).
  • ExoMars Trace Gas Orbiter: Detecting atmospheric gases on Mars (2016-present).
  • Artemis I: First uncrewed mission of NASA's Artemis program, orbiting the Moon (2022).
  • Chandrayaan-3: India's successful lunar lander and rover mission (2023).
  • Luna 25: Russian lunar lander mission (2023, failed landing).
  • OSIRIS-REx Sample Return: NASA's asteroid sample return capsule landed on Earth (2023).
  • JUICE: ESA's Jupiter Icy Moons Explorer launched to study Ganymede, Callisto, and Europa (2023).
  • Peregrine Mission One: First U.S. commercial lunar lander (2024, failed mission).
  • SLIM: Japan's Smart Lander for Investigating Moon, achieved precise lunar landing (2024).
  • Artemis II: Planned first crewed lunar flyby of Artemis program (scheduled for 2025).
