Jupiter

The gas giant with over 79 moons, including the famous Galilean moons that shaped planetary science.

Moons and Rovers

Jupiter has at least 79 moons, including the four large Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.

No rovers landed on Jupiter, but the Juno spacecraft has been orbiting Jupiter since 2016, studying its atmosphere and magnetosphere.

Learn about the Galilean moons | Learn about Juno

Suitability for Life

Jupiter's harsh conditions make it unsuitable for life as we know it. However, some of its moons, like Europa, may harbor subsurface oceans that could potentially support life.
